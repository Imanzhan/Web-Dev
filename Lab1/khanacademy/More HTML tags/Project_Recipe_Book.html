Beshbarmak

  • Time: 3 Hour
  • Serves: 1 persen
Ingredients Quantity
Horse Meat 1 kg
Flour dough 250 g
Onion 150 g
Potato 400 g
Ground black pepper 3 tablespoons
Salt 5 tablespoons

Step 1: Boil the horse meat and wait until it is cooked, about 2-2.5 hours.

Step 2: Remove the foam from the pan.

Step 3: Add the onion and potatoes to the broth.

Step 4: Wait until the potatoes are cooked and add the pre-chopped flour dough to the broth.

Step 5: After the dough is ready, carefully remove it and put it in a large bowl and arrange all the other ingredients nicely.
